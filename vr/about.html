What's a dib anyway?

A dib is simply a buyer's offer.

Our competitors flood sellers with comments and PMs from potential buyers.

We believe in simplicity. We provide the seller with a clean and concise list of dibz.
That way, the seller can easily pick the best offer.



When do I call dibz?

The buyer calls dibz when they want to make an offer on an item.

Our competitors do not give the buyer a uniform way of telling the seller they want an item.

We believe in consistency. We have each potential buyer fill out the same auction form.
That way, the buyer knows that the seller won't miss their dib.
